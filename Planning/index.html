Menu Guru

Welcome

Welcome to the menu planning app for your next camp, event or function. Add your information into the essential data forms, (dark blue boxes) then consult our information resources (green boxes) and change your stored version of the databases to hold your own meals (light blue boxes). Then you can save your work localy or in a specific directory where you can find it again and asks some of the very basic questions regarding your menu.

Let the scripts put it all together and print out the finished reports before you set off or as you go. I haven't managed to directly place your order with your supermarket of choice but it will give a comprehensive list that makes sure you stand a chance of not missing things out.

I know that cooking is as strange as the individual, and everyone has their own ideas about what should be in a scrambled egg, how long it should be cooked for, and what implements to use to do it. You can change the recipe book (light blue boxes) to make your own meals and allocate implements at will. This app is to help give you an easy guide to turning your meal for 1 into crates of ingredients, distructions & daily menus to go out to a patrol of 10 diners.

If you want to find out more about the assumptions behind this app (green boxes) then please follow the button in the navigation at the top of this page, to the resources section.

If you have previously saved your requirements please go to upload section, find your work so far and cook on sucessfully for ever.
